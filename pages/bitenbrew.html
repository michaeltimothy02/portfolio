Overview

Bite&Brew is an HCI project focused on designing a user-friendly mobile application for exploring food and beverage recipes. The name Bite represents delicious food, while Brew symbolizes refreshing drinks, reflecting the app's goal of bringing both culinary experiences together in one simple and enjoyable platform.

Design Process

The design process consisted of three stages: understanding user needs, organizing the app structure, and creating an interactive prototype.

1

Research & User Persona

Conducted a survey using Google Forms to understand user needs and preferences, then created user personas to guide the design process.

2

Information Architecture (Sitemap)

Organized the app's page structure and navigation to ensure a clear and intuitive user experience.

3

Prototyping

Created interactive designs and prototypes in Figma to visualize the user flow and overall experience.

Visual Language

The visual direction is inspired by the warmth of coffee shops and home cooking. A combination of earthy brown, soft beige, warm gold, and dark charcoal creates a cozy and inviting experience for exploring food and beverage recipes.

Typography uses Lora for the Bite & Brew brand identity, giving the app an elegant and welcoming personality. Poppins is used throughout the interface to ensure clear readability and a modern user experience.
