zoom_in Click to enlarge

Overview

Circlo is a personal project that focuses on creating a social platform where teenagers and young adults can connect through shared interests. Inspired by the idea of a circle, Circlo provides a space for users to join communities, interact with like-minded people, and build meaningful connections around their passions.

Tagline: "Where Circles Come Alive"

Design Process

The design process was structured into three key stages, each building upon the last to create a well-informed and cohesive user experience.

1

Research

Researched teenagers and young adults aged 15–30 who are active on social media and interested in joining communities based on shared interests.

2

Wireframing

Developed low-fidelity wireframes to quickly test layout decisions, content hierarchy, and navigation patterns before committing to visual design choices.

3

Prototyping

Built high-fidelity interactive prototypes in Figma to visualize the complete user journey and validate design decisions with real interactions.

Visual Language

The visual design of Circlo uses a violet-purple color palette with a soft lavender background to create a modern, vibrant, and welcoming experience for young users.

Circlo Purple #534AB7
Lavender Mist #EDE9F9
Mid Violet #7B74CC
Deep Ink #1A1830

The interface uses Poppins as the primary typeface because of its clean, modern, and easy-to-read appearance, making it suitable for a social platform aimed at teenagers and young adults.
